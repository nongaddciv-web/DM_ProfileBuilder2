Loading Assemblies

Load an Assembly

Click the 'Assembly Browser' button.
The Assembly Browser window will open.

Finding an Assembly

1. Click the Open Library button to choose a new libray folder.
2. The current folder is shown at the top left.
3. Enter text to filter results.
4. Click an assembly thumbnail to load it.
