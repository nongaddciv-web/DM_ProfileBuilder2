Stamp Profile Tool

Launch the Tool

1. Open the Profile Builder dialog.
2. Click the Stamp Profile Tool Button

Stamp the Selected Profile

After launching the tool, a component will be created of the current proilfe.
Click anywhere in your model to place the component.
The component will automatically align to any face in your model.
